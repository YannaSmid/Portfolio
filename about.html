Hi, I'm Yanna Elizabeth Smid

I am a Creative Technologist and Computer Scientist passionate about combining AI and creativity.

Educational Journal

In 2019, I started my bachelor's degree in Bioinformatics at Leiden University, which I finished cum laude in 2023. During this program I quickly began to realize that I enjoyed writing code, which I had never done before. Programming is just using mathematics to solve puzzles! Later on I found my interest in many areas within Computer Science, especially AI, Date Science, and Game Development. During my exchange at Ohio State University in 2023, I discovered how to merge Computer Science with my creative mindset, which inspired me to pursue more creative applications of technology.

This is what led me to the master’s program in Media Technology (now Creative Intelligence and Technology) at Leiden University, where I was able to combine my creative thinking with my interest in Computer Science. This program has challenged me to think outside the box and allowed me to explore areas such as game development, creative coding, and interactive systems using tools like Processing and PureData. It also gave me the flexibility to deepen my expertise in Data Science and AI, through courses like Deep Learning, Modern Game AI, Evolutionary Algorithms, and AI in Society.

Currently, I’m finishing up my thesis, which exploreed the application of Reinforcement Learning and Imitation Learning techniques in strategy games. Here I was able to train AI models for one of my favorite Nintendo video games, Fire Emblem.
