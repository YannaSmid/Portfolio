About Me

Thank you for checking out my portfolio webpage! Here you can find all my professional information. My name is Yanna Smid. In 2019 I started my bachelor's degree in Bioinformatics at Leiden University. Here I discovered my interest for programming and Data Science. After finishing my classes for Bioinformatics, I went to the Ohio State University where I further developed on my programming skills, and where I was able to use my interest in design and art. I discovered that science and creativity are closely related to each other, and that often you need one for the other. This is exactly what fits my interests, and what I want to work with. Therefore, I started my Master's degree in Media Technology or now called Creative Intelligence and Technology. Here I was able to use my creativity for scientific research and Computer Science. This program offered me to enhance my knowledge in Computer Science and use it for creative aspects. I am honored to show you the pieces that I have made during my academic path so far, and I am excited for what is coming next!
